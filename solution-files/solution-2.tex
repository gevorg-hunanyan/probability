\begin{sol}{twodice-5}
    Let $\Omega=\{1,2,\ldots,6\}^2$, $A=\{(5,5)\}$, $B=\{(1,4),(2,3),(3,2),(4,1),(4,6),(5,5),(6,4)\}$. We are required to find $\PP(A\mid B)$. Note that $A\cap B=A$, hence
    \[
        \PP(A\mid B)=\frac{\PP(A)\cap\PP(B)}{\PP(B)}=\frac{\PP(A)}{\PP(B)}=\frac{1/36}{7/36}=\frac17.
    \]
\end{sol}
